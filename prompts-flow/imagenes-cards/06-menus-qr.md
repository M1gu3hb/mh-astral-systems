# Menús QR interactivos

**Qué debe mostrar:** un menú digital que el cliente escanea con su teléfono, explora y
pide; se actualiza sin reimprimir nada.

**Prompt (pégalo en tu generador):**
```
A QR menu experience in dark 3D space: a phone panel scanning a glowing QR code, a beam of
electric-blue light opening a floating frosted-glass digital menu with abstract dish cards
(image thumbnail + price chip) organized in categories. A small table/plate hint gives
restaurant context. Feels instant, contactless and easy to update. Style: dark premium
glassmorphism 3D render, deep near-black navy background #070B16 with a soft radial
electric-blue glow and faint circuit/grid texture, frosted glass UI panels floating in 3D
with depth and soft shadows, electric-blue #1E5BFF and light-blue #5B8CFF accents, chrome/
silver #BFD6FF highlights, cool rim light, subtle bloom, clean minimal SaaS aesthetic,
cinematic, high detail. Only abstract UI shapes and simple iconography, no readable
paragraphs of text, no third-party logos. The QR code can be a clean abstract square code.
16:10 landscape.
```

**Negativos:** green, warm/orange tones, cluttered layout, paragraphs of text, gibberish
text, watermark, third-party brand logos, low-res, noisy, flat 2D clipart, faces in focus.
